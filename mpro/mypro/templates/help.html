    PROCEDURE

    STEP 1

    Diagnosis of Cancer-Type, Stage

    Preliminary step is to get checked and diagnosed for the type of cancer the patient is suffering from, along with the stage to be able to classify the test results in the future. The diagonsis done here is of the utmost importance as it determines the entire classification process. The classification here primarily are of 5 types of cancer: Lung Cancer (Non-Small Cell and Small Cell), Skin Cancer (Melanoma), Prostate Cancer, Breast Cancer, and Blood Cancer. The stages however depends on the type of cancer the patient is diagnosed with.

    STEP 2

    Filling of form and Blood Test

    The next step is to approach the clinic or the laboratory where a form will be filled along with the blood drawn. The blood test is given a ID which is the register ID and report ID. This process is different from the other kinds of blood tests as the prominence relies on the telomere length. Telomere is a bio-marker present in the tail of the DNA which has multiple functionalities. The first being an important component during DNA replication and joining of multiple DNA's into a strand. However, here it exhibits the health of the patient and a marker that shows the radiation units an individual can be exposed to. This, is the functionality the treatment relies on and what we measure during the analysis.

    STEP 3

    Measurement of Telomere and Analysis

    The images are generated which consists of the DNA from the blood sample colleceted. From this, the images are synthesised to get only the telomere highlighted. Telomere length is measured using a model and the length is taken as basis of classification.

    STEP 4

    Report on Personalized treatment method

    Measurement of the Telomere leads to the end of classisification process under the analysis. The results will give the insight on the various treatment methods that can be an option amongst the many with minimum side-effects and effective treatment with better recovery.

    STEP 5

    Recovery guide and Post-Treatment check

    A set of recovery methods are also suggested in the report with healthier lifestyle changes to be adapted. There will be post-treatment checks to check both the telomere length and whether there is any relapse of cancer so they can be treated at the earlier stages.
